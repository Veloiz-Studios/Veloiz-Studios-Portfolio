Last updated: June 2026. How we handle and protect your information at Veloiz Studios.

1. Information We Collect

When you interact with Veloiz Studios (by booking a call, sending an inquiry, or becoming a client), we collect specific personal and business information. This may include your name, email address, phone number, business details, and project requirements.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information exclusively to:

  • Communicate with you regarding your project inquiries.
  • Provide personalized web design and development services.
  • Process payments and manage billing.
  • Improve our services and website experience.

3. Data Protection and Sharing

Your privacy is important to us. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners or trusted affiliates.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party tools such as Calendly for appointment scheduling and Formspree for form submissions. These services have their own privacy policies governing how they handle your data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you. You may also request that we correct or delete any inaccurate or out-of-date information.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, the practices of this site, or your dealings with Veloiz Studios, please contact us at moizshaikh.develops@gmail.com.
